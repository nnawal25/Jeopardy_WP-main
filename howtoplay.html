How to Play Jeopardy

Jeopardy! is a popular American television game show that has been on the air since 1964. It features a quiz competition where contestants answer clues in the form of answers, and the correct response must be given in the form of a question. The show is known for its broad range of categories, including history, literature, science, pop culture, and more.

The steps are given as follows:

1. Split your group into anywhere from two to four teams.

2. Begin the game.

3. Choose one team to pick the first category.

4. Read the question and attempt to the it. You can choose to allow discussion between teammates or have the teams switch off individual players to answer each question.

5. If the correct answer is given, that team will earn the point value of that question.

6. If the wrong answer is given the team will not earn anything.

7. If the team gets it wrong, the teams are given a chance to answer.

8.The team who gives the correct answer gains control of the board and chooses the next category and point value.

9. Repeat Steps 4-8 until the board is empty.
